— Meet the Maker —

Hi, I'm Hoor

The hands behind every set, the heart behind every detail.

🌸

Welcome to my little world of nails! I'm Hoor, the creator and craftswoman behind every set you see here.

Nails by Hoor started as a passion project — I've always loved the way a beautiful set of nails could transform an outfit, lift a mood, or mark a special moment. What began as making pieces for myself and friends quickly became something more.

Every set I make is handcrafted with care in my home studio in Peshawar. No shortcuts, no rushed work. Each nail is shaped, painted, designed, and sealed with the same attention you'd want for your own special day.

My promise to every client: your set will be uniquely yours. Whether you want subtle elegance or full glam drama, I'll make sure your nails feel like a perfect extension of you.

Thank you for being here, for trusting me with this little detail of your day. It means more than you know. 💖

— Hoor 🌸
