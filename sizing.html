Sizing guide

The most important step! Accurate sizing ensures your press-ons fit like a dream.

📐 What you'll need

To measure your nails accurately, gather these:

  • A flat surface (like a table)
  • A bottle cap (any small bottle cap works!)
  • Your phone camera
  • 2 minutes of your time

📸 How to take your size (super easy!)

Our sizing method is simple — no measuring tape needed! Just follow these steps:

Step 1: Place your hand flat on a table or any flat surface.

Step 2: Keep a small bottle cap right next to your hand (this gives us a reference for accurate sizing).

Step 3: Take a clear picture of your four fingers (index, middle, ring, pinky) with the bottle cap visible in the photo.

Step 4: Now do the same for your thumb separately — flat on the surface, bottle cap beside it, take a clear picture.

Step 5: Repeat both steps for your other hand.

Step 6: Send all 4 photos to us on Instagram and we'll handle the rest! ✨

💡 Pro tips for the perfect photo

  • Good lighting matters: Take photos near a window or in well-lit room for clarity.
  • Flat surface only: No tilted hands! Keep your palm flat on the table.
  • Bottle cap visible: Make sure the cap is fully in frame and not too far from your hand.
  • Spread fingers slightly: So each nail is clearly visible without overlapping.
  • Both hands needed: Don't forget — we need photos of left AND right hand.
  • Save your sizes: Once we have your photos, future orders are super easy!

🌸 Need help sizing?

If measuring feels confusing, don't worry! Just DM us a clear photo of your hand on a flat surface with a coin or ruler next to it for reference. We'll guide you through everything.
