— The Process —

How to order

From your dream design to your doorstep — here's exactly how it works.

01

Tell us your vision

Slide into our DMs on Instagram @nails.by.hoor or fill out our order form. Share with us:

  • The vibe you're going for (bridal, glam, casual, themed)
  • Color scheme or mood board pictures
  • Inspiration photos (Pinterest, Instagram screenshots)
  • The occasion (wedding, birthday, Eid, just because!)
  • Any specific elements you want (rhinestones, charms, glitter, designs)
02

Send your size photos

Our sizing method is super simple — no measuring tape needed! Check our sizing guide for the full process. We'll need:

  • Photo of 4 fingers (flat on surface, bottle cap beside hand) — both hands
  • Separate photo of your thumb (same setup) — both hands
  • Preferred length (short, medium, long, extra long)
  • Preferred shape (almond, coffin, square, oval, stiletto)

Don't worry — we'll guide you through this step by step in our DMs!

03

Confirm design & payment

Once we have your sizes and design idea, we'll:

  • Send you a sketch or mood board to confirm the look
  • Share the final price including all customizations
  • Send our payment details (JazzCash, Easypaisa, or bank transfer)
  • Lock in your order once payment is received
04

We craft your set

This is where the magic happens! Each nail is hand-painted, designed, and sealed with care. Crafting time is usually 5–7 days depending on complexity.

  • Premium quality nail tips
  • Salon-grade gel polish for long wear
  • Extra attention to detail on customizations
  • Final quality check before packaging
05

Delivery to your door

Your set is carefully packed in our signature box with everything you need:

  • Your custom nail set (10 nails)
  • Application kit (glue, prep wipes, cuticle stick, mini file)
  • Application & care instructions
  • Shipped Pakistan-wide via TCS or Leopard Courier
06

Apply & slay 💖

Follow the included instructions to apply your set perfectly. With proper care:

  • Press-ons last 1–3 weeks per wear
  • Reusable up to 3–5 times with care
  • Don't forget to tag us in your photos!
