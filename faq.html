— Common Questions —

Your questions, answered

Everything you might want to know before placing your order.

How long do press-on nails last? +

With proper application and care, our press-ons typically last 1–3 weeks per wear. Some clients even get them to last longer! Application care matters: avoid soaking hands too much, wear gloves while doing dishes, and treat them gently for maximum wear time.

Are press-ons reusable? +

Yes! With careful removal and storage, you can reuse our sets 3–5 times. Each set comes with care instructions to help you make the most of them. We recommend using nail tabs (sticky pads) for shorter wears if you plan to reuse.

How long does it take to make my set? +

Standard turnaround is 5–7 days from order confirmation. Bridal and luxe sets may take 7–10 days due to detail work. Need it sooner? Add our rush option (3-day turnaround) for an additional fee. We'll always confirm timeline before you pay!

Do you ship across Pakistan? +

Yes! We ship Pakistan-wide via TCS or Leopard Courier. peshwar delivery: 1–2 days. Other cities: 3–5 days. Delivery charges are added at checkout based on your location.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept JazzCash, Easypaisa, and bank transfer (HBL, MCB, etc.). Full payment is required to start crafting. Cash on delivery is currently not available since each set is custom-made.

Can I return or exchange my set? +

Since every set is custom-made just for you, returns/refunds are not possible. However, if there's a quality issue or sizing problem on our end, please reach out within 48 hours of delivery and we'll make it right!

Will the press-ons fit my real nails? +

Yes — as long as you send clear sizing photos! Our method is easy: just place your hand flat with a bottle cap beside it and snap a photo. Check our sizing guide for the full step-by-step process.

What if my nails come off? +

Press-ons can lift if not applied correctly. The application kit includes glue and a prep wipe — make sure to clean and dehydrate your natural nails before applying. Press firmly for 30 seconds. If you're new to press-ons, watch our application tutorial saved on Instagram highlights!

Can I order matching sets for bridesmaids/groups? +

Absolutely! Group orders (3+ sets) get a 10% discount. Perfect for bridal parties, birthday squads, or matching with your sis. DM us with details and we'll send you a custom quote.

How do I take care of them? +

Each set comes with a care card, but the basics: avoid harsh chemicals, wear gloves for cleaning, don't pick or pry them off, and remove with cuticle oil + warm water. Treat them gently and they'll thank you!
